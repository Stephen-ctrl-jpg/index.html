ABOUT US

Our college was established in 1990 and has been providing quality education to students ever since. We offer a wide range of courses in various fields such as science, engineering, arts, and commerce.Our college is located in the heart of the city and is easily accessible by public transport. We have a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing the best education to our students.

OUR COURSES

Bachelor of Computer Science

                  

A Bachelor of Computer Application degree is the core of Computer Science in today's world. This degree is for those who want to study computer science, software engineering, information technology, information security, and networking technology. ranging from software development and artificial intelligence to cybersecurity and data analysis.

Bachelor of Commerce

                  

The B.Com courses list includes Marketing, accounting, entrepreneurship, various taxation and industrial laws etc. This holistic approach positions them for success in diverse fields, such as finance, marketing, and management, while fostering ethical and socially responsible leadership qualities highly valued in the competitive job market.

Learn More

Bachelor of Arts

                  

The most common undergraduate degree offered by universities and colleges worldwide. B.A. degrees are mainly awarded to students following a programme in the Liberal Arts (e.g. Humanities, Social Sciences). the BA program opens up diverse career prospects, ranging from journalism and publishing to counselling and public relations.

Bachelor of Business Administration
